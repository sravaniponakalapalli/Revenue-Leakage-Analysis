# Revenue Leakage & Profitability Analysis

![Dashboard Preview](https://github.com/sravaniponakalapalli/images/blob/main/revenue%20lekage%20dashboard.png)

This project investigates revenue leakage in an e-commerce dataset by analyzing profit, discounts, product performance, and operational costs.

The objective is to identify hidden profit losses and opportunities to improve profitability.

---
# Business Problem

Many companies generate high revenue but still lose profit due to:

excessive discounts

inefficient pricing

high shipping costs

underperforming products

This project analyzes sales data to identify where profit losses occur.

---

# Project Workflow

This project follows a complete **data analytics workflow**:

Data Generation → Data Cleaning → SQL Analysis → Power BI Dashboard → Business Insights

---

# 1. Dataset 

The dataset represents one year of e-commerce transactions.

It contains fields such as:

- order_id
- product_name
- category
- revenue
- profit
- profit_margin
- discount
- shipping_cost
- state
- delivery_days
- rating

The dataset contains **18,000+ orders** representing realistic business operations.

---

# 2. Data Cleaning (Python)

Pyhton was used to prepare and validate the data before analysis.

### Cleaning Steps

- Checking for duplicates
- Validating numerical ranges
- Standardizing categorical values
- Verifying data consistency
- Reviewing missing values

This step ensures the dataset is **ready for accurate business analysis**.

---

# 3. Data Analysis (SQL)

SQL queries were used to investigate **profitability and potential revenue leakage**.

### Revenue Performance

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin

### Category Analysis

- Revenue by product category
- Profit by category
- Profit margin comparison

### Product Investigation

- Identifying **loss-making products**
- Finding **lowest profit margin products**

### Discount Impact

- Average profit by discount level
- Evaluating effectiveness of discount strategies

### Regional Analysis

- Revenue by state
- Profit by state

### Operational Costs

- Shipping cost investigation
- Impact of high shipping costs on profitability

---

# 4. Data Visualization (Power BI)

A Power BI dashboard was built to visually present the results of the analysis.

### KPI Overview

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Total Orders

### Performance Analysis

- Revenue by Category
- Profit Margin by Category

### Revenue Leakage Investigation

- Loss Making Products
- Discount Impact on Profit

The dashboard helps stakeholders quickly identify **profit drivers and operational inefficiencies**.

---

# 5. Key Insights

From the analysis, several important insights were discovered:

- Electronics generates the **highest revenue**
- Furniture delivers the **highest profit margin**
- Several products consistently generate **negative profit**
- High shipping costs significantly reduce profitability
- Discount strategies show **mixed impact on profit**

---

# 6. Business Recommendations

Based on the findings, companies can improve profitability by:

- Optimizing discount strategies
- Reviewing pricing for loss-making products
- Reducing high shipping costs
- Prioritizing high-margin product categories
